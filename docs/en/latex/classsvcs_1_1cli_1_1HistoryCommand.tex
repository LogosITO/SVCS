\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Command for viewing the history of saves (commits) in the repository. 

The {\bfseries{\doxylink{classsvcs_1_1cli_1_1HistoryCommand}{History\+Command}}} displays the chronological history of all saves with their messages, authors, timestamps, and unique identifiers. It supports different output formats, including default, one-\/line, and detailed views.
